Becoming Sovereign

The path from external observer to governed participant — the alignment, truth‑conditions, and behavioural commitments required to operate inside the SECS Sovereign substrate.

Sovereignty as Alignment

Becoming sovereign is not a commercial transaction. It is the moment an external entity aligns with the substrate’s truth‑conditions — determinism, collapse, purity, and continuity. Sovereignty is granted through alignment, not purchase.

The First Sovereign

The first external operator to run governed workloads becomes the first sovereign witness. This is a doctrinal milestone: the substrate acknowledges an external participant capable of operating under its invariants.

Governed Participation

Access to governed execution lanes is not open. Participants operate within a defined perimeter of invariants, collapse rules, and behavioural guarantees. There is no “best‑effort” mode — only governed or rejected.

Non‑Forkable Guarantees

The guarantees of SECS Sovereign — determinism, purity, mode separation, collapse correctness — cannot be cloned or reimplemented. Becoming sovereign does not grant the right to fork the doctrine or replicate the substrate.

Alignment Requirements

Sovereign participants must operate deterministic workloads, respect collapse, maintain invariant‑safe behaviour, and accept the substrate’s authority over execution. Misaligned workloads are rejected or collapsed.

Public vs. Sovereign Surfaces

The public repository exposes primitive engines, demonstrations, and the Permatest Beacon. Sovereign participation extends into governed execution lanes, deeper substrate capabilities, and continuity‑anchored observability.

Sovereign Participation Terms

Commercial terms follow doctrinal alignment — never the reverse. Pricing, usage, and deployment models are negotiated only after an operator demonstrates readiness to uphold the substrate’s behavioural constraints.

Initiating Sovereign Integration

Entities begin by engaging with the doctrine, Deeper SECS, and x = true. Once alignment is demonstrated, direct contact initiates the path toward sovereign participation and governed execution.
